Aspirations

“Speech is power; speech is to persuade, to convert, to compel."  - Ralph Waldo Emerson

Apart from being the world famous host, I’m also planning to start an NGO which not only caters women right and serves as a tool to speak up for them in the society but also offers workshops for the general public to enlighten their confidence, public speaking, and critical thinking skills. Especially, for the disadvantaged children in our society who belongs to a lower social class or are disabled. Due to several reasons, they are often neglected in society and for that reason, they usually have lower self-esteem while speaking most importantly when they are speaking other than their mother tongue - Cantonese.
I believe in sharing the gift of speaking with others to do well and achieve highly. I plan to open up various speaking activities for the students who can better their skills on a new level. I also plan on to opening different workshops for people to learn more about the art of speaking in public and how to excel in it through the implementation of various techniques can help further enhance the speaking skills of theirs. There will be a blend of teaching techniques taught by me and various globally acknowledged world speakers that can impact to bring a change in the potential candidates speaking abilities.
Also, I hope that one day I would be able to speak for gender equality at an international level. In fact, would get qualify to become a United Nations Women Goodwill Ambassador just like my idol Emma Watson. Although many people are aware of such inequality such as wage gap or employment opportunities, not much difference has been made. Thus, through speech, great results can be achieved as Ralph Waldo Emerson once said: “Speech is power; speech is to persuade, to convert, to compel”. An example can be noted is the speech for HeForShe campaign of Emma Watson. I hope to spread my words and knowledge. In other specific events like the UNESCO press Conference, United Nations climate summit. Where I can use my skills to contribute to a social cause. These events can let me share my outstanding professionalism as a united citizen of the globe.
Other than volunteer work, I would like to work in Hong Kong specifically in the Big Fours namely, Deloitte, PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC), Ernst & Young (EY) and KPMG as a Marketing Specialist. Since I’m interested in this field and have completed a relevant degree. Thus, I would like to build on my career in this field other than being a freelance host and grow from these opportunities.
