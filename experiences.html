Experience

I first started hosting at the Grand Opening Dinner of Mentorship Programme 2016 for the Hong Kong Federation of Business Student. Through the valuable experience, I had a taste of what hosting is like and also the thrill and excitement the career brings. I then the privilege of hosting various international and grand events like the Hong Kong International Innovation and Entrepreneurship Exhibition 2017, the CityU Employers Luncheon, TEDxCityU, International night and the Columbia University Department of Computer Science Award ceremony. Through these precious experiences, I recognized my passion for hosting and therefore decide to take up new challenges in the form of hosting different kinds of events such as commercial activities and weddings.
I have worked with several international schools in Hong Kong as their master of ceremonies. I’ve worked with the French International School, Spanish Primary School, and German Swiss International School. Events that I hosted include but not limited to annual performances, graduation ceremonies, and festive fun galas.
 
Due to my multilingualism, I was therefore invited by several celebrities, for instance, Michelle Yeoh, Isabelle Huppert, and Claudia Wagner, to host for their public and personal events, such as engagement ceremonies and parties, weddings and birthday parties. Commercial-wise, I have hosted multiple and different kinds of press releases, such as local singers’ and international big names like Celine Dion’s music album press releases, movies premiere events for both international and local movies, for example Captain America: Civil War (2016) and Project Gutenberg (2018), and book publishing and related events during Hong Kong Book Fair in 2018: International Publishing Forum - The Global Trend of Publishing with the Application of Technology. Furthermore, in 2016, I was invited to host for Louis Vuitton Spring Fashion Show in six different languages including English, Cantonese, Mandarin, Spanish, German, and French. Also, I hosted the grand opening of Hong Kong Vivienne Westwood Cafe.
 
Being an activist on fighting for gender equality, I dedicated a huge part of my career to managing and hosting events related to feminism and women’s right. For 3 years in a role from 2016, I was invited to host for the International Women’s Day: Women Election Forum for Hong Kong Federation of Women’s Centre. Other than gender equality, I am also one of the youth members of Youth Development Commission of Hong Kong which they strive for a better connection between the Government and younger generation in Hong Kong for a better future. Because of that, I become a regular host for related events, for example, Youth summit and the 2017-18 Outstanding Youth and Outstanding Youth Activity Commendation Scheme award presentation ceremony held in October 2018.
From the above experiences, it is believed that I am a well-rounded host for various kinds of events such as entertainment, commercial, social or even political ones due to my versatility and my background.
