My Story 

I’m a hybrid Chinese and German. I’m a polyglot and can speak 6 languages fluently, including English, Cantonese, Mandarin, Spanish, German, and French. I have been hosting numerous events around the globe for the last 6 years. 

Over 10 internation events hosted

I've had the privilege of hosting various events over the years like the Hong Kong International Innovation and Entrepreneurship Exhibition 2017 to Celine Dion’s music album press releases and Captain America: Civil War's movies premiere.

Aspirations

Apart from being the world famous host, I’m also planning to start an NGO which not only caters women right and serves as a tool to speak up for them in the society and offer workshops for the general public to improve their confidence, public speaking, and critical thinking skills. 

I also aspire to be a United Nations Women Goodwill Ambassador just like my idol Emma Watson so I can increase awareness on issues such as the gender wage gap and employment opportunities for women.

I truly believe that through speech, great results can be achieved as Ralph Waldo Emerson once said: “Speech is power; speech is to persuade, to convert, to compel."
